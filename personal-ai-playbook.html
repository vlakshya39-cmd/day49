Welcome to your Personal AI Playbook

This is a private, offline toolkit for reusing your best AI prompts instead of retyping them. Everything here is built around how you actually use Claude: building single-file apps, job hunting, and documenting the #60DayClaudeChallenge.

Workflows & the Prompt Builder

Workflows are ready-made prompt templates grouped by what you're doing (App Building, Job Search, Content). The Prompt Builder lets you assemble a brand-new prompt from small labeled pieces — role, objective, context, and so on — with a live preview as you go.

The Loop Builder is for autonomous runs

Turn any one-shot prompt into a self-checking loop: Claude works, checks its own output against criteria you set, and keeps improving until a stop condition is met. Everything saves to this browser only — nothing leaves your device.
